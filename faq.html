FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

  1. Can I adjust the vibration on the Duo Wristwatch?
    Currently, the Duo Wristwatch offers a fixed vibration intensity. We designed it with an ideal balance of strength to ensure it's noticeable without being disruptive. If you need to check if the vibration is strong enough, you can easily test it by pressing the doorbell while wearing the watch.
  2. Is the Duo Wristwatch waterproof or water-resistant?
    Yes, the Duo Wristwatch is water-resistant, meaning it can withstand splashes of water or brief exposure to moisture. However, it is not fully waterproof, so we recommend removing the watch before swimming or showering.
  3. Can I use the Duo Wristwatch with other doorbells?
    No, the Duo Wristwatch is designed specifically to work with the Duo Doorbell. The system is tailored to communicate via a unique signal, ensuring that you only receive notifications from your own doorbell.
  4. How do I install the Duo Doorbell?
    The Duo Doorbell is designed for easy installation. Simply mount it near your door, and follow the included instructions for pairing it with the Duo Wristwatch. It’s a simple, DIY-friendly setup that doesn’t require any special tools or technical knowledge.
  5. How far is the range between the Duo Doorbell and Duo Wristwatch?
    The Duo Doorbell and Duo Wristwatch have a range of approximately 100 feet (30 meters) in a typical home environment. This distance can vary depending on walls and obstacles, but it should cover most residential homes comfortably.
  6. How do I reset or troubleshoot my Duo Wristwatch and Duo Doorbell?
    If you encounter any issues with the Duo Wristwatch or Duo Doorbell, try resetting both devices by following the troubleshooting guide in the user manual. If the issue persists, feel free to contact our customer support team for assistance.
